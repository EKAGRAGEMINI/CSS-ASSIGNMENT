Different Heights Of Lines!

Height:5px

Pixels(px) are relative to the viewing devices

Height:5em

Relative to the front-size of the element(2em means 2 times the size of the current font)

Height:5rem

Pixels(px) are relative to font size of root element

Height:5vw

Pixels(px) are relative to 1% of the width of the viewport*

Height:5vh

Pixels(px) are relative to 1% of the width of the viewport*
